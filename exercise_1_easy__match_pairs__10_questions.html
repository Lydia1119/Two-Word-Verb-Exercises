Exercise 1 (Easy) - Match Pairs - 10 Questions

Match Pairs

Match the following two-word verbs with their meanings.

Note that STH means something; SB means somebody.

JXUwMDFi 1. come along A. (+ on) get involved in an exciting or profitable activity/opportunity
JXUwMDE5 2. get in B. (+ to) succeed in reaching a physical destination or stage
JXUwMDFk 3. come over  C. come in the area near STH/SB
JXUwMDEy 4. go over D. be happening soon (esp. be broadcast soon)
JXUwMDEw 5. take in E. come to a place or area (spot, room, town, country), esp. towards SB or to join SB
JXUwMDFh 6. get through F. (+ to) reduce itself to one particular thing that is the most important or essential matter
JXUwMDFj 7. come up G. remove STH that was previously put up or put in place
JXUwMDFm 8. take down H. provide a place for SB to live or stay
JXUwMDEx 9. get off I. manage to avoid serious trouble or consequences (esp. legal punishment)
JXUwMDFl 10. come down J. move towards a place or person, esp. by crossing an area (room, city, country)

Enable JavaScript

Answer Key

1. C

2. A

3. E

4. J

5. H

6. B

7. D

8. G

9. I

10. F
